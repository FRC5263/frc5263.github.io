About Us

What We Do.

We build robots. Every year we compete in the FIRST ® Robotics Competition. In six short weeks, we design, build, and program an industrial-sized robot to compete in a unique game against teams all across the state.


Who We Are.

Our team is from Riverview, Michigan, established 2014. We're just getting started, and with every year of experience, we're only getting better. Our students take full control of building the robot.
